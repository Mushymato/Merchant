## Author's Guide

Although Merchant is not a framework mod, authors can interact with it by providing some specific data.

#### [`mushymato.Merchant/Customers`](./001-customers.md)

Adjust attributes related to normal customers.

#### [`mushymato.Merchant/ShopkeepThemeBoosts`](./002-shopkeep.md)

Define special boosts that can be applied to buildings to boost earnings from haggling.

#### [`mushymato.Merchant/TourismWaves`](./003-tourism.md) and [`mushymato.Merchant/Tourists`](./003-tourism.md)

Add and change logic for non-standard NPC customers.
